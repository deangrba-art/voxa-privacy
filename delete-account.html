VoXa Account Deletion

If you want to delete your VoXa account and associated data, please email:

support@computerteam.com.au

Please include the email address used in the VoXa app.

After we receive your request, we will delete your account data, login tokens, translation credits, subscription entitlement records, and related account information.

Some limited records may be kept if required for legal, security, fraud prevention, payment, or subscription record reasons.

You can also request account deletion from inside the VoXa app if the option is available in Settings.
